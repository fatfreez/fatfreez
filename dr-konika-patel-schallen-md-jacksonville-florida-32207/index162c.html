How can I fix my uneven gummy smile?
Uneven Smile Treated with Neurotoxin
Oct 1, 2020
A small amount of neurotoxin placed in the depressor anguli oris and the depressor labii inferioris can help relax and even out your smile. This can be achieved in small doses and might require subsequent treatments. Results are temporary and may only last 1-2 months.
I’m just wondering is it possible to get rid of scars left from childhood stitches?
Scar Treatment with Lasers and Microneedling
Oct 1, 2020
Old scars even from childhood can improve with lasers and microneedling. Lasers will help stimulate collagen to allow the scars to lay smoothly on areas of curvature such as the forehead. Microneedling with PRP will also create a more smooth, flat appearance of the scar. Scar therapies can be used to treat old and fresh new scars. New scars or injuries can be treated with laser therapy even the next day after suture removal.
How to treat my post acne and acne scars?
Acne PIE & PIH
Sep 24, 2020
PIE & PIH are very common after acne. In order to improve the erythema, I would recommend Vbeam, a pulsed dye laser, that is specifically designed to treat redness in the skin. For the PIH, I would recommend a combination of laser and some topical skin brighteners. Discoloration Defense by Skin Ceuticals does a great job at improving PIH when applied twice a day. A laser treatment such as, Picoway, would improve the acne scars and enhance skin tone as well as pore size.
