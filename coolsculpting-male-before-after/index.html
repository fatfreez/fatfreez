The need to be in the form often runs counter to the way of life: sedentary office work, frequency of business lunches and time trouble, hindering regular exercise.

Then you can not do without the possibilities of hardware therapy. One way to get rid of excess fat is cryolipolysis – removal of adipose tissue under the influence of cold. With the help of one cryolipolysis procedure for men, it is possible to reduce fat deposits (in the correction area) to 30%. During 2 months, the process of losing weight started during the procedure will continue.

Men also want to look good, especially in the abdominal area. CoolSculpting for men is an excellent option to eliminate excess fat. Also cryolipolysis is used to remove excess fat in the chest, with gynecomastia.

Dual Sculpting For Men

During the cryolipolysis session, the fat fold is captured using a nozzle that is exposed to cold. Under the influence of low temperature fat cells fall into the process of apoptosis – the process of programmed cell death. Thus, cryolipolysis stimulates the natural disappearance of adipose tissue. Cold impact is only on the fat tissue inside the nozzle of the device, without affecting the rest of the body – this process is controlled by a special system of sensors. The procedure is painless (only a slight numbness in the area of the device) and very practical – it takes only 30 minutes, during which you can read, view documents or work with a laptop.

Some men find it difficult to remove fat in problem areas. When you can not get rid of fat with exercise and proper nutrition, you can try CoolSculpting treatment.

Men Want To Look Great Too

Cryolipolysis is ideal for those people who value their image, comfort and time.

Indications for the procedure:

  • Abdominal fat deposits;
  • Fat deposits in the inner thigh area;
  • Fat deposits in the buttocks area;
  • Fat deposits in the back area.

Contraindications to the Coolsculpting procedure:

  • Cold urticaria
  • Areas of impaired peripheral circulation
  • Raynaud’s disease
  • Extensive scar tissue in the treatment area
  • Skin diseases: eczema or dermatitis in the area of presumed exposure
  • Skin Sensitivity Disorders
  • Injuries and skin infections
  • Areas of recent bleeding or hemorrhage
  • Pacemaker
  • Paroxysmal cold hemoglobinuria.

Advantages

Why Men Are Choosing Coolsculpting

Non-invasiveness. The method of cryolipolysis is also called “non-surgical liposuction.”

The absence of recovery period. Immediately after the procedure, you can return to the usual routine of the day.

Painless. All that the patient feels is a pleasant chill and temporary numbness of the treated area.

Versatility. The procedure of Coolsculpting is equally effective for both women and men.

Efficiency. It is scientifically proven that the patient gets rid of 1 cm in volume for 1 procedure.

No stretch marks and / or flabby skin. A similar effect occurs with a sharp weight loss, when the skin does not have time to adapt to sudden changes in body volume. The process of destruction of fat cells is gradual, the final result can be seen after 2-3 months.

Dual SculpSure for men

Safety. The temperature of the impact in the applicator, the monitoring of body temperature throughout the procedure, as well as the duration of the exposure – all this controls the device, so that the procedure is absolutely safe.

If the body temperature drops by at least half a degree from the programmed temperature, the procedure will be interrupted.

The Zeltiq device today is about 10 years of clinical research, 4 FDA approvals (the US Department of Health Agency), over 400,000 successfully performed procedures in America and about 1 million procedures worldwide.

Periodicity

The number of procedures is determined by the doctor, starting from the patient’s initial data: the physique features, the fat fold thickness of the desired result. Visible result becomes visible after 3-4 weeks, the full effect can be estimated after 2-3 months. The interval between the procedures is 2 months.

Thus, the CoolSculpting for men is most often used to get rid of fat on the abdomen, arms, and excess fat in the male breast.
