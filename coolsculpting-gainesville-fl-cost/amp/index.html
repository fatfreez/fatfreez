How is the procedure performed, what are indications and contraindications to Coolsculpting.

Contraindications:

– pregnancy, lactation;
– diabetes;
– oncology;
– Diseases and skin lesions at the site of localization;
– cold intolerance (for example, cold urticaria is an allergic reaction to cold);
– autoimmune diseases;
– increased vascular permeability, telangiectasias at the area of exposure;
– absence of fatty folds or their small thickness;

What discomfort can be during and after the Coolsculpting procedure?

At the beginning of the procedure, you can feel discomfort, especially if the fold is not supple and needs a stronger vacuum that would draw the fold.

Then there is a warm-up and when the cooling begins – the sensations are different for everyone, but after five to seven minutes most people stop feeling this place and starts to doze, listen to music or read a book.

Immediately after the procedure, you can be very surprised by the “piece” of your fat which is blue-red “brick” rises above the abdominal surface. After stroking and kneading in a few minutes everything returns to normal.

Sometimes there may be a slight loss of sensitivity, itching is a short-term, reversible effect caused by the effects of cold on the epidermal nerve fibers. We do not recommend on this day to go straight to the beach or to the sauna.

Is it possible to do Coolsculpting for men?

You can, and it’s just as effective. According to statistics in America, the proportion of men is tens of percent. Moreover, there are already mentions that the technique can be applied to the treatment of gynecomastia (breast enlargement in men by female type).

But this requires further research and consultation with other doctors. Obviously, without fear, the technique can be applied only to the so-called false gynecomastia (lipomastia) – when the increase in the area of the breast is due to the growth of adipose tissue, and not actually the mammary gland.

How often should I repeated Coolsculpting procedure? Can I combine coolsculpting with other procedures?

Visible result may appear in two to three weeks. The final result can be seen after 2-3 months. From 20 to 40% of the volume of fat tissue is removed. The main thing here is the visual effect.
