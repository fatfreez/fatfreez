Can a doctor explain the physiological reason why someone should not bend over and lie down after Bot
Movement after Botox injection
Dec 8, 2015
Botox is reconstituted in different amounts of solution. It is presumed that it can migrate up to 1cm after injection site. In the crows feet area, this is not as concerning, however over the eyebrow, where we inject very close to a nerve responsible for keeping the eyelid open, this could be aesthetically unpleasing if the product drifts into that nerve causing Ptosis of the lid. We ask that you stay upright, no exercise, no hats and no hairbands to reduce the risk of this...
