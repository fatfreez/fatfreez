I am afraid of swelling and looking really bad for days. Is that an option with Voluma?
Voluma compared to Radiesse for Cheek Augmentation
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,I have worked with both and Voluma looks and feels more natural than Radiesse.  Voluma does not cause much swelling.  At the time of your injection, you can ask your provider to show you how it looks as they work so you can determine how much you need.  As with all fillers, it is better to do less and build on a foundation at future visits than to do too much and try to correct it.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Can getting fillers put in your cheeks cause any problems with your eyes?
Will fillers in my cheeks affect my eyes?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,If the filler is placed too high, it can cause narrowing of the eye aperture.  If you experience any sudden or increased visual changes, you should contact your provider so they can correct it or refer you to a specialist such as an opthalmologist or oculoplastic surgeon.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Will Emsculpt for buttocks regime permanently destroy all the fat there?
Will Emsculpt destroy my buttock fat?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,Protocol 2 on the Emsculpt is designed specifically for the buttock to tone the muscle but not to reduce the fat.  The difference between the two protocols on the software is scientifically determined for the improvement of the target area.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Does Emsculpt tighten skin? Does it help with cellulite?
Does Emsculpt tighten skin?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,Emsculpt is FDA cleared to tone muscle.  Anecdotally, we have seen leg skin tighten and cellulite improve after Emsculpt.  The mechanism of action is probably as the muscle firms and lifts, the fibrous attachments to the skin lift also.Warmly,Dr. Liu
I had bad results from Smart Lipo on my abs and have scar tissue inside. I want to try Emsculpt to break
Will Emsculpt break up scar tissue?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,The mechanism of Emsculpt is to tone muscle but not to break up scar tissue and it doesn't have FDA clearance for that.  If in an abdominal muscle treatment, you softened the scars, it would be a bonus.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Does Emsculpt actually make you stronger?
Does Emsculpt make me stronger?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,Emsculpt is FDA cleared for muscle toning.  If that muscle that is toned is in the thigh, it should increase the strength in that thigh.  Make sure you don't have any metal in the hip or knee in the treated thigh.Warmly,Dr. Liu
I want to do Emsculpt for the butt, but don't want to lose the little fat I have. How do I do that?
Will Emsculpt cause me to lose buttock fat?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,There are two protocols are on the machine.  Protocol one is specific for the abdomen to lose fat and tone muscle whereas protocol 2 is specifically for the buttock to tone and not to lose fat.  There is going to be a release of additional protocols and applicators for arms and inner thighs this fall.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Can you use Emsculpt if you have metal tooth implants or gold teeth?
Does Emsculpt affect metal teeth implants?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,Metal teeth implants are not a contraindication to Emsculpt since they are far away from the Emsculpt paddles.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Is Emsculpt safe for someone with an SVT (Supraventricular tachycardia)?
Can I get Emsculpt with SVT?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,The safest answer to your question is to ask your cardiologist and to talk to BTL, the manufacturer of Emsculpt.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Emsculpt perform by the specialist or staff?
Who performs the Emsculpt?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,The Emsculpt is performed by staff who have been trained by BTL the manufacturer, and a physician is around for supervision.  In our office, it is performed by a PA or RN.Warmly,Dr. Liu
Can I use Emsculpt if I have a bladder sling?
Can I get Emsculpt with a bladder sling?
Apr 17, 2019
Hi,You shouldn't get Emsculpt over any area of implanted metal.  Probably the safest way to check is to ask the physician for a copy of the physical lot and serial number of your sling if it is artificial.  You can contact the manufacturer directly to ask if there is any metal in your sling.Warmly,Dr. Liu
What fillers to balance long face?
What fillers to give volume?
Jan 16, 2019
Hi,Great question!  You definitely can contour your face to look more balanced.  The choice of fillers is made upon seeing your face in person, close-up and feeling the elasticity of your skin.  Voluma is a great foundation filler for restoring volume naturally.  It can also be placed with a cannula to recontour the jaw.  Best of luck!Warmly,Dr. Liu
If I stop taking Spironolactone, is it possible that my hormonal acne will come back? Please help me!
Will my acne be worse after stopping spironolactone?
Jan 16, 2019
Hi,Female hormonal acne responds exceptionally well to spironolactone.  There are many studies documenting how well it works when other acne medications have failed.  One alternative we have in our office is a prescription topical spironolactone in a cream for patients that don't want to take an oral.  We have found this to be very effective in patients that have hormonal chin acne.Warmly,Dr. Liu
What treatment should I get for redness? V Beam, Microneedling, Erbium?
How to treat red scars
Jan 16, 2019
Hi,Great question!  The best laser is the Vbeam for redness since is the number one vascular laser designed specifically for vessels and redness.  If it is shallow acne scars, you can definitely improve it with a series of microneedling with minimal downtime.  Erbium is when patients can afford more downtime and want a bigger result.Warmly,Dr. Liu
How long do I have to wait to get my cheek filler dissolved?
When can I have hyaluronidase?
Jan 16, 2019
Hi,Generally, we advise patients to wait 2-3 weeks to filler to settle before dissolving.  At this point, you provider can dissolve the excess Voluma.  Due to its chemical nature, Voluma generally requires 2-3 sessions of hyaluronidase to fully dissolve.  Best of luck!Warmly,Dr. Liu
