Coolsculpting is a non-invasive, painless method of getting rid of fat under the influence of cold. More and more people want to resort to it nowadays.

Local cooling of body parts will allow physiologically, without stress on the body, to lose weight effectively and quickly.

At the same time, there are no painful sensations, it is not necessary to resort to surgery, to expose your health to the risk of complications.

The essence of cryotechnology

Employees of the clinic at Harvard University, have developed a technology based on the principle of natural dying of fat cells under the influence of low temperature.

Adipose tissue is a source of energy for the body, when cooling, as a result of the splitting of fat cells, the release of heat energy begins.

Best Coolsculpting In Denver

As a result of the splitting of subcutaneous fat, excess pounds are gone, and several zones can be treated simultaneously during one session. Treatment of one zone takes an average of 1 hour, a vacuum applicator is applied to the treated area,which captures the fat fold.

Inside the applicator there are cold-generating plates that provide deep cooling of the fat fold. It is also worth noting that the sensitivity of muscles, nerves and vessels to such temperatures is significantly reduced.

Cold is felt only in the first couple of minutes after the procedure, after which it disappears. After removing the applicator, the area of the skin swells a little and looks reddened, reddening occurs the next day. Correction areas:

How does cryolipolysis work?

The technique is characterized by complete safety, to prevent hypothermia between the applicator and the skin is placed a special gasket, which ensures a uniform distribution of the cold.

The fat fold is cooled over the entire area, resulting in the fats being efficiently broken down by being processed in the process of metabolism.

CoolSculpting Targets Only Fat Cells

The body’s defenses are aimed at heating the chilled area, the cleavage process is absolutely safe for the surrounding tissues – vessels, nerves, muscles and skin. The procedure does not require a recovery period.

Effect

Unlike the traumatic methods of getting rid of fat, in cryolysis, rapid results should not be expected. The cleavage of fat cells in this case is natural, one session allows you to get rid of 20-30% of fat cells. The process of fat disintegration lasts for 60-90 days.

Depending on the individual characteristics of the organism and wishes, 1-3 procedures will be required with an interval of 1-1.5 months, the first results will be noticeable during the first month. The recommended course of the coolsculpting consists of three procedures with a one-month break.

After completing the full course, deep mechanisms of fat burning are started, the volume of fat cells decreases within nine months after the completion of the course.
