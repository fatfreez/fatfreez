Contraindications

  • violation of blood circulation in the treatment zone,
  • take of anticoagulants,
  • hypersensitivity to cold (cold urticaria),
  • impaired skin sensitivity,
  • fresh injuries, wounds, scars and other skin lesions in the treatment area,
  • hernia,
  • diabetes,
  • neuropathic disorders,
  • pacemaker,
  • pregnancy and the period of breastfeeding,
  • fat folds less than 2.5 cm thick.

How many procedures will it take?

To achieve a pronounced and satisfactory result, 1 procedure is sufficient. The course can be from 3 to 5 procedures individually, according to the indications.

How often can cryolipolysis be done?

After 21-28 days, the procedure can be repeated locally to enhance the effect in problem areas.
