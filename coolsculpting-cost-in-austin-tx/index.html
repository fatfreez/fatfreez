What is the difference between CoolSculpting devices?

It is difficult for an uninitiated person to understand the jungles of competing proposals and in what cryolipolysis system is really effective.

Many devices differ from each other not only at the price of the device itself, with its parameters, thanks to which a guarantee for success can be given, and accordingly the cost of the procedure.

The main differences between the devices are as follows:

  • The ability to maintain a constant low temperature on the cryoplates of the vacuum applicator, which allows you to achieve the desired effect in the depth of adipose tissue. In this case, temperature fluctuations are unacceptable;

    • Cool Sculpting Targets Fat Cells Alone, Eliminating Them In An Easy, Non-intrusive Fashion

  • The device has several applicators of different sizes for therapy in different parts of the skin;
  • The quality of the vacuum unit, which must produce a constant negative pressure (for a large applicator to minus 50 mm of mercury and an average applicator to minus 80 mm of mercury), because a vacuum is necessary to disrupt the blood supply of fat cells;
  • Integrated vibration technology minimizes the pain effect during the session and produces lymph drainage at the end of the procedure.

Before talking about the results of this method, you need to understand what actually happens in the layers of the skin during and after therapy.

How does cryolipolysis work?

At the beginning of the session, the fat fold on the problem area is sucked by the vacuum applicator inside the nozzle and is subjected to intensive cooling between the two cooling plates at minus 5 degrees Celsius. With the help of a special protective pad impregnated with a special gel, the overlying layers of the skin are reliably protected. The effect of low temperature on lipocytes (fat cells) becomes a trigger mechanism, as a result of which these fat cells enter the state of apoptosis (the natural process of cell death). “Frost” and vacuum slow the flow of blood to the fat cells and cause a natural loss of viability of adipocytes.

When will the results of the procedure be visible?

This process, as a rule, takes from 6 to 8 weeks. First, clothes will become more free, as the amount of subcutaneous fat will decrease by 25-27%.

Some owners of such devices promise patients a reduction in the fat layer to 30-35%, which is not confirmed by numerous scientific studies, as well as our extensive experience.

How long does the CoolSculpting session last?

The session lasts 60 minutes and it is absolutely comfortable. During the procedure, you sit or lie, drink tea or read, work on a computer or sleep lightly. After the end of the procedure, you can immediately return to the rhythm of your daily life.

Can the result be improved by repeating the procedures?

One procedure is sufficient to achieve an expressed result. However, in the problematic areas, the procedure can be repeated after 30-45 days.

The most stable areas are the fat deposits on the back. Here, it is possible to repeat the procedure of cryolipolysis up to three times. Areas of the abdomen and legs are considered the most favorable for this therapy.

Who is the best candidate for CoolSculpting?

Patients of all skin types not suffering from any chronic diseases and having small limited fat deposits.

Which parts of the body are suitable for the Coolsculpting treatment?

Unaesthetic fat deposits in the abdominal area (especially the fat apron), waist, sides, buttocks, on the back, areas of the inner and outer thighs (breeches), fat above the knees, internal surfaces of hands, fatty overhangs in the scapula.

Can Coolsculpting replace liposuction?

During Coolsculpting, fat cells are not completely destroyed, but only a small fraction of cells are stimulated to self-destruct – apoptosis. The thickness of the fat layer gradually decreases. This method is very good for small body fat.

During liposuction opposite, the fat cells are eliminated mechanically. Therefore, coolsculpting is not a substitute for liposuction.

In addition, the effect of skin tightening, which is present after liposuction, does not accompany cryolipolysis.
