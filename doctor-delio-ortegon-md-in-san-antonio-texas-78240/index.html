Breast rejuvenation after weight loss
Jan 11, 2019
Many patients experience a loss of positon and volume of breasts with weight loss. Yes you can have youthful appearing breast again! However, you will need to have a brest lift and augmenation.  Good Luck

Breast lift v. implant
Jan 11, 2019
You have a very common question.  In my experience most patients with your level of ptosis (sagging) are best served by the combination of a lift and implant.  This combination gives the longest lasting youthful appearance.  Good Luck

Is tummy tuck with flank liposuction dangerous? Better to do separately?
Lipo and tummy tucks
Oct 30, 2018
This is not generally a dangerous combination. I feel that this is a very powerful and underutilized combination for recontouring the waist and abdomen with the additional benefit in additional perceived buttock projection without buttock augmentation.
Should I get an umbilical hernia repair or abdominoplasty?
umbilical hernia and tummy tuck
Oct 18, 2018
You have a very common question. Yes umbilical hernia repair and abdominoplasty are often don at the same setting. If your surgeon is trained in general surgery, he/she may very often fix the hernia without the involvement of another surgeon.  This should not be an issue, however discussing your surgeons comfort and familiarity with this repair is important.
Is 300cc or 350cc saline better for short petite frame?
Implant decisions
Oct 18, 2018
Thank you for your question.  Your descriptions are very helpful.  Given that the implants that you are choosing from  have very little different in fill volume (25cc), I would go with your gut feeling. If you are drawn to the larger size then by all means go with that. In my experience, when patients are choosing between implants of very similar size it make sense to choose the larger.Good Luck
Am I a good candidate for a dual plane breast augmentation to avoid a breast lift and scars?
Lift v. dual plane breast augmentation
Oct 2, 2018
Given your pics it does seem that your best long term result will be from getting a mastopexy and breast augmentation.  Yes a dual plane 3 may given you a improved nipple position in your lateral photo, however the larger implant and extended dissection needed will result in a breast that is lower in your chest wall and not a perkier youthful appearance
Excision or liposuction?
liposuction v. excision
Aug 21, 2018
Given the size of the area in question and the appearance of you skin. I would strongly consider using no excisional attempts for treatment first.  I have seen very good results in this area with lipo and with coolsculpting.
Right implant still too high almost 6 months later. Will they settle?
implant settling
Aug 21, 2018
Thanks for your question. Given you photos it does appear that the right implant is slightly higher. Given that you have a well healed capsule (scar around the implant) by now. I would not expect the implant to move on its own. You should discuss a revision with you BCPS.
Is bruising and ecchymosis normal after liposuction?
bruises after lipo
Aug 21, 2018
From you pics it appears that you have had some injury to your skin. This may be related to blood supply or burn. You should definitely be caring for that skin with an ointment and discussing his with your surgeon. Lack of care of this skin injury may worsen the pigmentation irregularities that may result from this injury.
2nd breast procedure now have animation deformity according to my doctor. Why wasn’t this a problem b
animation deformity
Aug 21, 2018
From what you have indicated it sounds like your first saline implants may have been placed on top of the muscle, thus increasing the rippling you saw. If the new implants were placed under the muscle then the animation deformity you see is quite common.  You should speak to you PS and get more information.

restoration after reduction
