How does the procedure work?

You are comfortably located on the couch, the doctor installs an applicator that matches the chosen zone. The vacuum applicator captures the fat fold, and the cooling panels with liquid nitrogen deliver the cold to the tissues. Applicator apparatus is designed in such a way that provides high-precision cooling, aimed only at the fat fold.

The procedure lasts 60 minutes.

At the end of the procedure, the doctor performs a massage of the affected area. Massage restores blood circulation and relieves light numbness after exposure to cold.

How does cryolipolysis work?
