Coolsculpting Prices Raleigh

Cellulite and fat deposits are the most complex cosmetic problems that, at times, do not disappear even after lengthy physical training and hard diets. Coolsculpting is a fairly new but effective method of correcting the body shape, during which fat cells are destroyed by cold.

Difference from surgical liposuction

Unsuccessful attempts to get rid of excess fatty tissue often lead women to a plastic surgeon.

Surgical liposuction involves surgery, that is, there is always the risk of damage to nerves and blood vessels. The operation is performed under general anesthesia, so in addition to the additional burden on the heart and brain, it becomes necessary to undergo recovery.

24 Year Old Woman Treated With CoolSculpting Of Chin By Dr Benjamin Wood, MD, FACS, Raleigh-Durham Plastic Surgeon

Tissues are not damaged during cryoliposuction, since cold gas – a mixture of air and liquid nitrogen – is directed to a specific area of the body and acts through the skin. The device is equipped with cooling panels with a vacuum element that pulls the fat fold inside, where the fat freezes. The duration of the cold “bath” is 40-60 minutes. The procedure is absolutely painless, but feels like a walk in the frosty air. 3-4 sessions are required to completely remove body fat.

Features of the action

Regardless of whether the area of the buttocks is cooled or the fat is frozen on the stomach, the result will not be noticeable immediately. After the procedure, the patient feels a slight tingling and itching, redness appears on the skin, which very quickly disappear.

The decrease in body weight and volume will become noticeable in two to four weeks. The course of coolsculpting treatment can be repeated to improve the effect.

A Safe Method Of Fat Elimination

Coolsculpting can be combined with other cosmetic methods. The effectiveness of complex individual programs is 45% higher than local ones.

The safety and effectiveness of the method is indicated by the fact that treatment by cold is indicated to people with obesity of the endocrine and hypothalamic (associated with the lesion of the central nervous system) character. The range of indications includes a correction:

  • hips;
  • things area;
  • buttocks;
  • internal and external surface of the hands;
  • abdomen and sides;
  • knees;
  • area of the scapula.

Advantages

Coolsculpting helps effectively combat problems that could previously be eliminated only through surgery.

A very important point is that with the gradual destruction of fat cells (as occurs in cryolipolysis), there is no stress on the liver and heart, so the correction is not accompanied by side effects.

CoolSculpting Is A Safe, Non-invasive Procedure To Reduce Fat In Common Trouble Areas

Advantages of coolsculpting in comparison with the surgery:

  • painlessness;
  • with time the effect is enhanced;
  • without complications;
  • It does not require rehabilitation;
  • in total you will need 4 sessions with an interval of one month to get the effect in one zone;
  • there is the possibility of simultaneous cooling of paired areas of the body (thighs, hands);
  • affordable price.

Contraindications

The method of freezing fat cells has a number of contraindications:

  • pregnancy and the period of breastfeeding;
  • pathological obesity;
  • allergy to cold (hives);
  • disorders in the circulatory system;
  • injured skin in the correction area;
  • catarrhal infections in the acute stage;
  • presence of a pacemaker;
  • chronic skin diseases (eczema, dermatitis).
