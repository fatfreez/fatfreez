Coolsculpting procedure

The vacuum applicator is superimposed on a predetermined area of action.

Between the applicator and the skin is placed a special gasket, which protects the skin from hypothermia and ensures uniform lipolysis.

With the help of vacuum, the fat fold is drawn into the applicator and cooling is applied. During the procedure, you feel a slight coolness, and thanks to the constantly maintained vacuum, cooling is not local, but in the entire thickness of the fold.

Depending on the thickness of subcutaneous fat and wishes, one to three procedures are required with a period of one and a half months.

Possible side effects and contraindications

Coolsculpting by Zeltiq also requires the availability of a suitable state of human health.

The main contraindications:

  1. Allergy to cold and inflammation in the area of the procedure
  2. Infectious Diseases
  3. Chronic diseases affecting the endocrine system
  4. Autoimmune diseases
  5. Raynaud’s disease
  6. Pregnancy and breastfeeding
  7. Hernia in the treatment area
  8. The fat layer is less than 6.5 centimeters.
