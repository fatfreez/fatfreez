Is it normal to feel a little bit of discomfort after a tummy tuck?

Nov 17, 2015
It is normal to have some discomfort after a tummy tuck, particularly if the rectus muscles were plicated. Sometimes as patients get back to a normal exercise routine they notice some increase or a change in the type of discomfort.
