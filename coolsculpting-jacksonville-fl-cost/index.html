The magic remedy for weight loss is called cryolipolysis (from the Greek “cold” and Latin “fat dissolution”).

Cryolipolysis today is a progressive method of maintaining the harmony of the body, which the stars of the first magnitude trust in their bodies.

Madonna, Britney Spears and Demi Moore are on the list of fans of cryolipolysis. Agree, it’s impressive!

The secret to the effectiveness of coolsculpting is the increased sensitivity of fat cells (adipocytes) to a decrease in temperature, which is why an irreversible process of natural destruction, apoptosis, is triggered in them.

Part of the contents of cells is converted into energy, and some are excreted from the body by the liver and kidneys.

This process resembles the accelerated decay of aging cells, so it proceeds absolutely naturally, without inflammatory reactions, intoxication and other side effects.

Chilling The Tissue In The Treatment Area To A Specific Tenperature

And as the number of adipocytes in the body of an adult body is no longer increasing, new cells are not replaced in any way destroyed – the results of cryoliposuction with a reasonable diet and active lifestyle will not last long, but for life.

Consider what happens during the cryolipolysis procedure. First of all, problem areas are defined – location of adipose tissue, where it is possible to easily fix a pleat with a thickness of 25 to 60 mm.

This fold is caught in the working nozzle, where it gradually cools. At this temperature, the self-destruction processes described above are activated in the fat cells.

In this case, the effect of low temperatures is limited by the fat fold – the probability of supercooling of muscles, internal organs, joints is reduced to zero, and the skin easily and without consequences transfers such short-term cooling.

The procedure of cryoliposuction itself takes only 20-90 minutes, it is perfectly tolerated, does not require moral preparation, anesthesia, and you can immediately return to your usual life.

During the session itself, at first, there is a feeling of pressure due to vacuum and tingling immediately after the cooling panels are turned on, after a few minutes if the sensations are replaced by numbness that disappears a few minutes after the end of the coolsculpting treatment.

These 20-90 minutes of the cryolipolysis session are sufficient to trigger the large-scale destruction of fat cells in the area of exposure, however, do not wait for immediate results.

The decrease in volumes becomes noticeable only after 30 days, and the maximum effect is achieved in 2-3 months – the volume of adipose tissue in the affected areas is reduced by 20-50%.
