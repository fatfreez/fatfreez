CoolSculpting For Submental Fat (double Chin)

The CoolMini has provided great non-invasive results under the chin. CoolSculpting provides approximately a 25%-30% fat reduction per area. You will need to consult with a local provider to determine if you are an appropriate candidate for this procedure.

The provider will also be able to discuss expectations surrounding results. (Janet Allenby, DO, Delray Beach Dermatologist)

CoolSculpting is a great treatment to reduce the double chin fat in appropriate candidates. However, not everyone is a suitable candidate for the treatment. We have had great success using CoolSculpting in combination with several other double chin reduction treatments we have at our practice.

The key is that you make sure you are under the care of an experienced physician who has multiple options for treating your concerns. Make sure you get an in-person consultation and evaluation to determine which treatments would be most appropriate for you. (Michael A. Zadeh, MD, FACS, Sherman Oaks General Surgeon)
