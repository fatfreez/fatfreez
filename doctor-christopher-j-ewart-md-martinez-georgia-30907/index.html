How Long Does Tummy Tuck Surgery Swelling Last?
Swelling after tummy tuck
May 30, 2019
Tummy tuck swelling can most likely be seen or located in the lower abdomen and/or the mons area post-operative. A majority of the swelling can last up to 6-12 weeks. To help alleviate swelling, you can rest, elevate lower extremities, and ice areas. Keep in mind that it can take up to 6-12 months for best results and all swelling to be resolved.

2 months after arm lipo and I'm still so tight and sore. Is this normal?
Liposuction
May 30, 2019
It is normal to still have tightness and soreness s/p liposuction to the arms. Liposuction can take a couple of months to heal from. It is important to make sure you are wearing your compression garment as your surgeon has directed. You can also do mild arm exercises to help regain your strength in your arms and to also loosen the tightness in your arms. If the tightness persists despite 3 months s/p liposuction, it is best to follow up with your plastic surgeon.
