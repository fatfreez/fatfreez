How is the Cooltech procedure carried out?

The procedure is practically painless, because some clients manage to take a nap.
The doctor applies a thermal compress with a gel-like mass to the problem area.This allows the skin to quickly moisturize, which is necessary for better response to temperature. In addition, the nozzle is a heat conductor that improves the combustion of fatty tissues.

Preparation: what should be done before the procedure?

After making a decision to undergo cryolipolysis procedures, a mandatory examination and consultation with several medical specialists is required in order to exclude possible side effects.

Stages of the Cooltech procedure

  • The client is located on the couch.
  • The gel base is applied to the required area.
  • The required area of the skin is captured by means of an applicator, to which cold will be directed.
  • Throughout the session, a specialist monitors the quality of the density of the applicator being contacted, the temperature regime.
  • After the procedure, there may be light bruises that go through a couple of days.
  • End of the session: the nozzle with the gel is removed, the client is free and can do the usual things.

What can and can not be done after the Cooltech procedure?

After a session for a while you can not drink, since liquid can settle in fat-free places. You can not take a hot bath and a shower, this reduces the rate of disintegration of fat after frostbite, which threatens the low efficiency of the method.

It is required to reduce physical activity, since it is fraught with the onset of headache and slowing down of metabolic processes in the body.
A second procedure can be carried out after 30 days.
