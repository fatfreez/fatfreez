Coolsculpting Zeltiq is a method of cooling fat cells, which reduces the number of these cells in the treated areas by approximately 20-30%.

FAQ

What does patient feel like during Coolsculpting procedure?

At the very beginning of exposure, the patient feels cold. However, less than 7 minutes from the beginning of the session, these discomforts disappear, and the procedure continues in a comfortable mode.

Are there CoolSculpting Side Effects or Risks?

As side effects of cryolipolysis, sometimes there are reddening of the treated area, changes in the sensitivity of the skin, and minor pain sensations. These manifestations do not prevent a patient from immediately returning to social activity and disappearing after a few hours or 1-3 days after exposure.

What happens to skin following the Coolsculpting procedure?

The skin on the treated area can turn red and become less sensitive. This does not require additional physician intervention – after a few days these side effects completely disappear. If we talk about any specific effect of cryolipolysis on the skin condition, then it is not.

Some patients even before the start of therapy are wondering what to do with “excess” skin, if it appears after the removal of fatty deposits … You will not encounter this problem if:

  • the area requiring processing is very small,
  • you are young, and your skin has good elastic properties.

It’s another matter when the elastic properties of the skin are initially reduced (due to age, specific condition of the tissues) and it is planned to remove a large amount of fatty deposits.

In this case, the second stage of correction can be shown – the application of procedures, restoring the turgor of tissues, for example, RF-lifting.
