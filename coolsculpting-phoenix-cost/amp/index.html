The procedure Coolsculpting on the Zeltiq device means “sculpting with cold”. Based on the method of cryoliolysis (“cryo” – cold, “lipolysis” – split), the procedure allows you to get rid of local fat deposits through exposure to low temperatures.

The secret of efficiency is simple: for the first time, weight loss occurs not at the expense of reducing the size of the adipocyte (adipocyte), but by destroying it. Scientifically proved that as a result of prolonged cooling, the process of apoptosis – natural cell death – starts.

As a result of the procedure, fat cells die and are removed from the body in a natural way. For 1 hour of the procedure the amount of adipose tissue in the problem areas is reduced by 1 cm.

How does cryolipolysis work?

37 Year Old Woman Treated With CoolSculpting Chin By Dr Andrew Wolin, MD, Phoenix Plastic Surgeon

The doctor is studying the features of the body, the location of the fat traps and the thickness of the subcutaneous fat layer in the patient.

Based on the results of the examination, the doctor makes a plan for therapy. The patient is placed on the couch, one of the five applicators of the Zeltiq apparatus is placed on the problem area. Anesthesia is not required.

The applicator captures the fat fold inside with a vacuum and cools it to a certain temperature strictly controlled by the apparatus. The entire procedure takes 1 hour.

Indications

The main condition for carrying out the cryolipolysis procedure is the presence of a fold of the fat layer with a thickness of at least 2.5 centimeters.

Excessive fat deposits on the abdomen, back, waist, hips, legs and arms;

55 Year Old Woman Treated With CoolSculpting Flanks By Doctor Steven Sorr, NMD, Phoenix Physician

  • Cellulitis;
  • Correction of body shape and weight loss.

Contraindications:

  • Injuries of the skin and exacerbation of skin diseases;
  • Individual intolerance to cold;
  • Pregnancy and lactation;
  • A number of chronic diseases;
  • The use of the pacemaker.

Preparation

Coolsculpting procedure can be carried out at any time of the day, regardless of the last meal and exercise before visiting the cosmetology room. The patient before cryolipolysis leads a normal life. The preparatory stage is reduced to visiting the doctor to identify possible contraindications and to choose the type of vacuum applicator.

Stages of the Procedure

Body Contouring Without Surgery And Downtime

The session takes about an hour. The impact consists of several stages:

First, the doctor measures the fat fold, wchich subject to correction with the help of a special apparatus. After measuring the area of exposure, it is circled with a marker and selected the type of applicator that is optimally suited for processing the problem area.

After this, the doctor applies a conductive gel on the marked area and fixes the applicator on it.

The device captures the crease with the help of a nozzle and starts cooling to about 5 degrees below zero. The effect of cold lasts 50-60 minutes. This time is enough to trigger the mechanism of apoptosis.

In the process of cold exposure, the device simultaneously makes vibromassage to prevent violations of the capillary circulation in the area of exposure and the surrounding tissues. After the end of the session, the applicator and gel remnants are removed from the skin.

Side effects

Coolsculpting is non-invasive procedure, but has a number of side effects of a local nature:

  • Temporary sensation of numbness in the cooling region;
  • Decrease of sensitivity in the treatment zone;

  • Possible redness of the skin or formation of bruises due to vacuum.
  • Special treatment of side effects is not required, they disappear within a few weeks.

Consequences of the procedure on counterfeit devices or doctor’s mistakes may be more serious: deep bruises, circulatory disorders and frostbite of the treated area.

You can be recommended the following procedures to enhance the effect of coolsculpting:

  • mesotherapy;
  • lymphatic drainage massage;
  • vacuum-roller massage;
  • RF-lifting.

Effect

In one session, the fat fold can lose up to 30% of the volume. To completely get rid of fatty deposits it is usually required to undergo a course of 3-4 procedures with an interval of 2 months. If the patient is not satisfied with the result, the course can be repeated, but not earlier than 4 months after the last session.

Changes in volume become noticeable 2-3 weeks after the session. The rate of disintegration of adipose tissue and the removal of its products depends on the individual characteristics of the organism, therefore the timing of the appearance of the results fluctuates quite strongly.

The final results become noticeable 3-4 months after the end of the course of coolsculpting.
