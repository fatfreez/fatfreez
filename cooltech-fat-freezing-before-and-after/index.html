PRINCIPLE OF COOLTECH

A Controlled Freezing System With A Non-invasive, Safe And Effective Method

The principle of the procedure is the selective effect of cold on the fatty layer. That is, you will feel only a slight numbness in the working area of the device CoolTech. The innovative method is based on precise and controlled cooling of adipose tissue, which leads to the gradual death of fat cells. The safety of the method is based on the special sensitivity of the fat cells to the cold.

Adipose cells fall into apoptosis, as a result of which they are destroyed, and after the procedure within two to three months they are removed from the body with the help of the lymphatic system. Only subcutaneous fat cells are exposed to cooling, surrounding tissue is not damaged.

On consultation before the procedure, the specialist determines your problem areas, then selects the appropriate applicator to process the fat folds. A cooling gel is applied to the treatment zone to protect the epidermis from the cold burn.

CoolTech Fat Freezing Consists In Freezing The Fat Tissue

The fat fold is captured with the help of the CoolTech cryolipolysis device. The nozzle is adapted in this way: selectively controls the cooling of fat cells, thereby destroying them.

During the procedure, you will be cold for the first 2-3 minutes, then you will feel absolute comfort.

In one session maximum, you can process three zones. Depending on the area of “freezing”, the procedure lasts from 45 to 70 minutes. During the year, one zone is recommended to be treated 4 times. The interval between the procedures for the same exposure zone is from 6 to 8 weeks.

About five days before and after CoolTech treatment, can not be sunbathing, nor should it perform procedures, that irritate the skin. For example, laser procedures or chemical peeling.

The main advantage of CoolTech is a pronounced local effect, manifested in the abdomen and thighs. This is the most problematic zone for women, because fat on the hips and abdomen “melts” very slowly. Our patients like that the effect is noticeable after the first procedure. In one cryolipolysis session, up to 30% of fat deposits in the correction zone is removed. If necessary, after a month and a half you can repeat the procedure, which will lose up to 50% fat.

CoolTech Treatment Is Around 60 Minutes

The effect of the procedure is prolonged, which does not cause stress in the whole body – it gathers speed as a snowball. In two months your body shape will become tempting. CoolTech, like Pygmalion, creates beautiful bodies, akin to a sculptural creation.

Indications for the procedure:

  • Abdominal fat deposits.
  • Fat deposits in the buttocks.
  • Fat deposits in the back area.
  • Fat deposits in the inner and outer thighs.
  • Fat deposits on any part of the body: abdomen, waist region of upper limbs: scapula, shoulders, hands, back.

ADVANTAGES:

  • The procedure is not associated with damage to the skin, does not cause complications.
  • The procedure works locally, does not affect the internal organs.
  • Does not require preparation.
  • Anesthesia is not required.
  • There is no rehabilitation period.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

  • Cold urticaria;
  • Areas of impaired peripheral circulation;
  • Pregnancy and lactation;
  • Extensive scar tissue, open wounds, hernia in the treatment area;
  • Skin diseases: eczema or dermatitis in the area of the intended impact;
  • Skin sensitivity disorders;
  • Injuries and skin infections;
  • Areas of recent bleeding or hemorrhage;
  • Pacemaker;
  • Paroxysmal cold hemoglobinuria;
  • Benign tumors and oncological diseases;
  • Violation of blood clotting;
  • Areas of impaired peripheral circulation;
  • Raynaud’s disease.
