The inner and outer thighs can be treated

There is a special applicator for the outer thighs, the inner thighs can be treated with a traditional applicator with some degree of overlap.

Results are great but you will need more than 1 treatment. Many people may need > 2 treatments to see permanent results. (Sameer Bashey, MD, Beverly Hills Dermatologic Surgeon)

CoolSculpting Treatment On Flanks And Inner Thighs Before After

CoolSculpting has an applicator specifically designed to treat the thighs, both outer and inner. Generally you can expect the same kind of results from the thighs as from other areas of the body – around a 25-30% reduction after one session.

CoolSculpting for thigh area

CoolSculpting is FDA approved to treat inner and outer thigh areas for fat reduction/contouring. To achieve the desired results, the CoolSculpting applicator needs to be able grab enough skin/fat tissue.
