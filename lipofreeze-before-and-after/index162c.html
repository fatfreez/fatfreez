LipoFreeze Before And After Photos

LipoFreeze is an innovative device that performs controlled selective cooling of adipose tissue, which allows to reduce its volume with a high degree of safety and comfort.

This cooling is possible by using a special vacuum applicator, into which the fat fold is captured during the procedure.

Cooling occurs immediately from both sides of the fold. In addition, the vacuum creates a temporary slowing of blood circulation inside the fold. This approach makes it possible to effectively cool subcutaneous fat without damaging the skin surface.

Cryolipolysis favorably differs from traditional methods of hardware and injection reduction of body fat, as it excludes trauma to the skin surface, fibroplasia and scarring.

As a result of a gradual soft decrease in the volume of adipose tissue, there is no systemic effect on the body and stress on internal organs.

One hour procedure Lipofreeze starts the processes of natural death (apoptosis) of fat cells, which lead to a 20-25% reduction in the thickness of the fat fold.

Cryolipolysis, Completely Destroys The Fat Cell

Advantages:

  • Safe: no side effects, common to most other methods of fat removal;
  • Effectively: stimulates a consistent and predictable reduction in fat in the affected area;
  • Performs a selective effect on fat without damaging other tissues;
  • Reduces fat deposits when other methods do not work.

The essence of the methodology:

The method is based on prolonged hypoxia and lowering the temperature of subcutaneous fat to +25 degrees, which subsequently leads to the natural death of fat cells – apoptosis.

This physiological process lasts for 50-70 days. The unique technology of cryolipolysis completely excludes cold damage to the skin, nerves, vessels and internal organs, since the freezing temperature of triglycerides is higher than the freezing temperature of other biological fluids and substances.

How does the Lipofreeze procedure work?

Fat Freezing Is Different To Other Non-surgical Lipo Treatments

The vacuum applicator is superimposed on the problem zone. Between the applicator and the skin is placed a napkin, impregnated with a special solution, protecting the skin from overcooling. With the help of vacuum, the fat fold is captured inside the applicator and cooled for an hour.

Result

Reducing the number of fat cells by 25% per procedure in the area of exposure. The effect of the procedure manifests itself within 1-2 months.

Depending on the thickness of the subcutaneous fat and the wishes of the client, one to three procedures are required with a periodicity of 2 months.
