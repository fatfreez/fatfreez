Cryo Fat Freezing Before And After

Cryotherapy is a unique technique for the effect of low temperatures on the body.

Cryo Fat Freezing is an effective non-surgical method of fat removal due to a local reduction in the number of adipocytes – the cells that form the basis of fat tissue.

Lymphatic drainage, anti-cellulite and lifting effects arise due to vibrational massage and the start of natural compensatory mechanisms in response to cold stress.

Cryotherapy procedure

Before the session, a specialist measures the thickness of the fat fold to automatically select the procedure for the procedure.

Then, cooling applicators impose on the problem area, which act through a protective cryotherapeutic napkin impregnated with lotus extract.

In the first minutes, the fat fold adapts to a decrease in temperature, tingling is possible for 5-7 minutes, then discomfort disappears.

The selectivity of coolsculpting is explained by the high sensitivity of fat tissue to cold in comparison with other tissues.

CoolSculpting, Also Called Cryolipolysis, Is A Medical Procedure That Helps Get Rid Of Excess Fat

Immediately after the session, the cosmetologist performs a light massage of the treated area. Special care is not required.

The only restriction is to exclude thermal effects (hot bath, sauna, solarium) for 3 days. The entire procedure takes 40-50 minutes, regardless of the number of zones.

The total duration of the recommended course is 8 weeks. During this time, you undergo 4 sessions with 2-week intervals. During one session, you can simultaneously act on several zones from 2 to 4.

The flat form of applicators allows you to work on any areas: abdomen, hips, buttocks, hands and work with fat folds of any thickness – from 2 cm.

Uniqueness of Cryo Fat Freezing in comparison with other systems is that it can be applied on the delicate skin of the inner surface of the thighs and hands.

Cryo Fat Freezing Non-surgical Treatment Aims To Remove Fat Cells From Problem Areas

The effectiveness of Cryo Fat Freezing is confirmed by clinical studies. After 4 procedures, the volume of the problem area is reduced by 3.5 cm. The maximum result recorded by clinical trials is 9 cm after 4 procedures.

It should be taken into account that the fat cells are gradually destroyed and removed from the body through the lymph and blood for 3-4 months. After the completion of the course of procedures, the effect of volume reduction will increase over the next 3 months.

Contraindications to the Cryo Fat Freezing:

  • pregnancy and lactation;
  • diabetes;
  • neoplasms;
  • Raynaud’s disease;
  • hernia in the affected area;
  • presence of an electrocardiostimulator.

Is It Safe To Freeze Away Your Fat Cells

Cryo Fat Freezing supports and enhances the effect of such procedures as:

  1. classical and lymphatic drainage massage (LPG);
  2. lymphatic drainage wraps;
  3. mesotherapy;
  4. pressotherapy;

These procedures can be performed one week after Cryo Fat Freezing procedure.

Cryo Fat Freezing is a unique method for eliminating excess fat deposits. This process occurs under the influence of low temperature and as a result the amount of fat tissue decreases.

The process of destruction of fat cells will continue after the course of procedures for several months.

Thanks to the cryolipolysis procedure, you can easily cope with local fat deposits, even those that can not be eliminated with a strict diet and intensive physical exertion.

What is important is that the result persists for a long time only under the condition of proper nutrition and regular physical exertion.

Slimming Down By Freezing Your Fat Cells Is Actually Feasible

Indications for carrying out Cryo Fat Freezing are undesirable fat deposits, and the best effect can be achieved if this procedure is applied in the abdomen, waist, back, knees areas.

For the procedure, two types of vacuum action nozzles are used: small and large. A large-sized nozzle is treated with large areas on which significant fat deposits accumulate, for example: the abdomen, back, waist, and thighs.

A small nozzle is used for hard-to-reach areas, such as the inner parts of the knees, thighs, hands and for places where fatty tissues are insignificant in volume. The duration of the procedure is 45-60 minutes. After Cryo Fat Freezing procedure must be done lymphatic drainage massage.

Sometimes, after the procedure, numbness due to cold exposure, slight itching and slight redness are possible, but such manifestations quickly disappear and do not affect the habitual way of life.

Cryo Fat Freezing has a number of advantages, namely, safety, painlessness, non-invasiveness, lack of toxic effect, high accuracy of cold exposure to fat deposits.

During the procedure, there is no injury to the skin, only at the initial stage there may be uncomfortable sensations.

Cryo Fat Freezing is contraindicated during pregnancy, breast-feeding and if there are different kinds of cold intolerance.
