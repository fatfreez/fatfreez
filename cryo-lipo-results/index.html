Cryolipolysis. How does it work?

Cryolipolysis on Cryo Lipo device in the clinic is a controlled cooling of adipose tissue. Correction of the body is as follows:

Fat fold is captured in a special vacuum applicator, due to which it is cooled immediately from two sides.

CryoLipo Lowers Temperature Of Skin And Fat

  • Low temperatures cause death of fat cells, since they are not adapted to long-term cooling. As a result, they break up into separate bodies (this process is called apoptosis).
  • Vacuum exposure enhances the effect, as it disrupts the circulatory processes in the fat fold.
  • Double exposure (low temperature and vacuum) destroys fatty tissues from the inside.
  • The products of decay are excreted from the body independently through the lymphatic system.

Safety

A specialist working on the Cryo Lipo apparatus only affects fat cells in a certain area. The surrounding tissues are not affected, so they are not destroyed or damaged.

Today, cryolipolysis is recognized as one of the safest hardware procedures for modeling a perfect body.

Principal features of the correction of the body shape with Cryo Lipo.

Innovative Procedure For Local Fat Removal

The device can be customized to suit the personal characteristics of each patient: the parameters are selected based on its physical characteristics and wishes for the result.

Cryo Lipo is:

  • 10 levels of vacuum strength;
  • 2 vacuum operation modes: continuous or pulsed;
  • wide operating temperature range.

If necessary, the specialist simultaneously uses two applicators at once, so in a single session, several zones can be processed, using their own parameters for each. This reduces the time of the procedure and the number of visits to the doctor.

The procedure is painless (usually the patient feels tingling and tingling) and absolutely safe, moreover – the impulse effect of the vacuum positively affects metabolism and lymphatic drainage.

The procedure will be appreciated by everyone who wants to become the owner of a beautiful body, but does not want to resort to surgical methods, chooses comfort, safety and result. Stages of the cryolipolysis procedure:

The Fat Cannot Return To Reside In Fat Cells That Have Disappeared

The doctor applies a tissue napkin to the treatment area, pre-soaked with gel. This avoids skin damage and frostbite during the session.

Depending on the task, the specialist selects the required applicator or two, sets the parameters of the procedure and begins to influence the fat fold.

During cooling, which lasts no more than an hour, the fat-destroying processes begin. Dead fat cells are removed from the body naturally through the lymphatic system. This process takes 4-5 weeks, after which the procedure can be repeated. The duration of one session takes about one hour. The result reaches its maximum in a few weeks after visiting the clinic.
