Coolsculpting Abs Before And After

Cryolipolysis is a non-invasive procedure that very effectively eliminates excess fat deposits, including those in areas of the body that are difficult to correct by other methods.

Coolsculpting is the most effective modern method of combating fat deposits in the waist, abdomen, buttocks, hips and hands.

The purpose of this technique is not only the correction of problem areas, but also the safety of the entire human body as a whole: cryolipolysis has an effect locally, without damaging the surrounding tissues.

It is proved that fat cells are more sensitive to cold. Cooling leads to apoptosis of fat cells, resulting in apoptotic “bodies”, which are subsequently absorbed by macrophages.

Thus, the extracellular matrix is not affected. The procedure of cryolipolysis lasts approximately one hour.

CoolSculpting Is The Latest And Greatest For Fat Reduction And Body Contouring

Is Cool Sculpting For Abs safe?

This procedure is absolutely safe, because there are no invasive interventions, anesthesia is not needed, blood cholesterol level does not increase, the lymphatic system is not overloaded.

Methodology.

A special napkin is applied to the treated area, then the manipulation of the working nozzle starts, in which a negative pressure is created to capture the fatty layer, which in turn makes it possible to effectively act on the fat cells.

Then it starts warming up to + 37 ° for 10 minutes, and then – gradually cooling down to about -10 ° C for one hour. In one session, you can treated to two zones. If necessary, the procedure can be repeated in a month.

Procedure protocols.

Reasons To Choose CoolSculpting

Cellulite treatment: vacuum 30 kPa, warming to + 37 ° C, further cooling to + 5 ° C.

Fat deposits, with the thickness of the fat layer 25-35 mm: vacuum 40 kPa, warming up to + 37 ° C, and cooling to -5 ° C.

Local fat deposits, with a fat fold thickness of 35-55 mm: a vacuum of 50 kPa, warming up to + 37 ° C, and cooling to -10 ° C. The free program allows the specialist to choose the necessary vacuum power and the cooling and heating temperature independently.

Results of the Coolsculpting treatment on abs.

The effect of cryolipolysis has a prolonged effect: the process of “splitting” lipids occurs within six months of the first procedure.

After the cryolipolysis procedure.

There may be redness, slight bruising is possible, and the sensitivity of the skin is reduced. All side effects in most cases occur within a few days after the procedure.
The advantages of the procedure.

The device for the procedure of cryolipolysis is equipped with a heart rate sensor, in order to monitor the client’s well-being (because the procedure is long), as well as an emergency STOP button to immediately stop the exposure. This technology is awarded the biggest award – the Nobel Prize!
