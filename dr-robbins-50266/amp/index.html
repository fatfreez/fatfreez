Can anyone tell me the difference between Vaser Liposuction and Smart laser liposuction?
Vaser vs laser liposuction
Sep 10, 2018
VASER uses ultrasound energy to break up fat and is a very efficient means of removing fat especially in areas where the fat may be more dense.  Laser liposuction uses a laser to produce heat and laser energy to the fat.  I primarily use it for small areas where there is a small amount of fat and mild skin laxity.  The heat of the laser may give a tightening effect to the skin.  David Robbins, MD, FACS

BBL risks
Sep 9, 2018
Fat transfer to the buttocks is a safe procedure.  However, just as with any surgical procedure, there are risks that need to be discussed with your surgeon.  Fat embolism occurs when the fat is injected too deep and the fat enters a deep blood vessel and travels to the lungs.  The fat should be injected in a superficial plane in the buttocks to avoid this potential complication.  Dr. David Robbins
