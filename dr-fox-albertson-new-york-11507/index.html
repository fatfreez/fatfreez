My lips are uneven after masseter Botox. What options do I have?
Uneven lips
Mar 5, 2019
A proper Botox injection at the masseter should not cause an uneven lip. I advise you to please follow up with the practitioner who did your Botox injections to figure out the cause of the uneven lip.
Can I do Botox the same day if I'm a ballet teacher?
Botox injections and ballet
Mar 4, 2019
For a person to receive the best and longest results with Botox injections, it is best to avoid any aggressive physical exercise for the 48 hours following the Botox injections.
The reason for this is that Botox blocks the signals in the neuromuscular junction to prevent wrinkles triggered by muscle movement. Vigorous excise within 48 hours may decrease the blocking effects of Botox injections.

Products safe for pregnancy to address hyperpigmentation and acne?
Pregnancy and acne

Unfortunately, treatment options are very limited during pregnancy for acne and hyperpigmentation. Acne wise there are 2 prescription medications that are safe during pregnancy. One being erythromycin gel or solution and the other being Azelaic acid in a gel or foam.

Using Benzoyl Peroxide and Vitamin C

Is it normal not to have inflammation/swelling after CoolSculpting?
Post Coolsculpting Treatment
Feb 11, 2019
After coolsculpting most patients will feel numb and tingle in the area. Occasionally there can be some black and blue. Inflammation may or may not happen, but if it does it may occur a few days after the treatment. It will take up to 3 months to see the results of the treatments.
Does massaging your treatment area daily help CoolScuplting results?

Jan 11, 2019
Massaging the treatment area is only proven to aid in the fat loss when done immediately after the coolsculpting treatment. Although it most likely is not harmful to massage the area in the days following your treatment, there is no scientifically proven benefit in doing so.
