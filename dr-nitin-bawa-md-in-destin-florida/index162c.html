What are the pros and cons of dermabrasion for wrinkles, pigmentation and pores on Indian dark skin?
Dermabrasion for Indian Dark Skin
Sep 21, 2020
Dermabrasion removes dead skin from the surface using different types of machines. It does not go very deep at all so cannot help with wrinkles. Since it only removes skin that is already dead, it does not affect the melanocytes that are deep down that produce pigment. It also would not affect pores although by smoothing out the surface of the skin some people might feel that their skin feels more even.
