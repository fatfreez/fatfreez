Will Breasts Sag if I Decide to Explant?
Sagging breasts after implant removal
Sep 25, 2017
If your skin is of good quality, then removing the implants should not cause drooping.  Implants replace volume and fill up the skin envelope. When the volume is removed they will be less full for sure.  In some cases a lift would be recommended if the nipples are below the inframammary crease.  I would seek the opinion of a board certified plastic surgeon and have him or her assess your tissue.  They may recommend a wait and see approach (staging) or an immediate lift.
