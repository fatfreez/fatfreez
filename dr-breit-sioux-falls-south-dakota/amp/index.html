Can I have a breast reduction while overweight?
Breast Reduction
Mar 20, 2018
It is possible to have a breast reduction while overweight. The most ideal situation would be if you could get down to your ideal weight and then have a breast reduction. If you have a breast reduction and then lose weight, you are taking the risk of seeing your breast size decrease further or become droopy. There are some insurance companies that will require a minimum trial of conservative treatments, including weight loss, and must be documented by a physician. Check with your insurance...
