Can CoolSculpting cause fat killed off from treated areas to redirect to the breasts?
Coolscupting kills fat
Jul 26, 2018
Thank you for your question. Since Coolsculpting destroys the fat cells treated, they cannot not be redirected to your breast tissue as they no longer exist. If you do notice your breasts becoming larger after Coolsculpting it is most likely due to weight gain or is hormone related.
