Fat Freezing With Zeltiq Coolsculpting

Zeltiq coolsculpting is a procedure that seeks to eliminate fat cells in the body through the concept cryolipolysis. You can freeze fat away with Zeltiq coolsculpting by involving no needles, no pain and no anesthesia. The fat freezing procedure was first discovered by two dermatologists known as Dieter Manstein and Rox Anderson who were of the Wellman Center for Photomedicine.

How does Zeltiq work?

Zeltiq has adopted the use of cold therapy for the purpose of freezing fat cells which actually kills them. The procedure is totally harmless as it works without causing any damage to the surrounding tissues.

Cold Based Technology Uses An Applicator That Is Applied To A Specific Area And Cools The Fat Cells

The procedure involves the use of a non invasive applicator which delivers a precisely controlled cooling that eliminates body fats.

The area that has these body fats is usually suctioned between two cooling plates: bringing the temperature around the fat cells just above freezing point. This causes a slow collapse in the fat cells which results in their death.

So what actually happens to these dead cells? The body usually absorbs these dead cells and eliminates them naturally through various metabolic processes. This can take a course of two to three months.

Who is a good candidate for this procedure?

The procedure is best fitted for those people who have stubborn fat areas that just canât seem to go away no matter how much they exercise they engage in.

CoolSculpting Is A FDA Approved, Non-surgical, Treatment For Reducing Unwanted Fat

There are certain fat bulges that are never easily reduced through exercise or diet. Zeltiq cool sculpting works best for these types of fat areas.

It is also best fitted for people who seek to eliminate specific fat areas such as the tummy. It is important to note that the procedure is not an alternative for weight loss; it just seeks to freeze excess fat away. It is never used as a substitute of invasive methods like liposuction.

Are there any risks involved?

The Zeltiq cool sculpting is a non invasive procedure that is said to be harmless. However there are minor side effects that have been associated with the procedure. These include redness of the skin and localized bruising. These are effects that usually clear after a few weeks. Patients may also experience a dulling sensation in the treated area.

Does Zeltiq Coolsculpting really work?

Clinical studies have proved that cool sculpting procedure results to a measurable and noticeable fat reduction in patients. This change is fully noticeable after a period of four months. For the purpose of achieving greater results the procedure can be administered for more than four months.
