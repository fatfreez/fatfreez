CoolSculpting and Fillers
Jul 29, 2020
You can have filler placed on your chin the same day since CoolSculpting on the “chin” is actually done on the “double chin”. The double chin is actually not a chin, but the fat deposits below the anatomic chin, and this area is called the submentum. CoolSculpting is done on the submentum and other fat deposits of the neck, so there is no problem with getting filler and CoolSculpting on the same day. Best wishes, Mary Beth Mudd, M.D.
