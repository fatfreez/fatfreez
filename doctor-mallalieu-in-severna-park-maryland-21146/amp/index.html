Fine lines on the inner corner of the eye – Any advice?
Eye Wrinkles
Feb 24, 2020
Wrinkles around the eye will be treated primarily with Botox or similar product. These will improve the wrinkle and also prevent it from getting worse with time. From the photo you appear to have good skin quality so small treatments like RF microneedling or light laser treatments will keep your skin looking great. Best, Dr. M
What number of grafts would you estimate I would need?
Graft Volume
Feb 20, 2020
There is such as thing as too much at once! Grafts must develop blood supply to survive and thrive. Grafts can be placed too close together and they end up not doing as well. For this reason, along with your photos I’d say 2500-2750 grafts placed primarily in the frontal region. Best of luck, Dr. M
Can I get a black and white tattoo of a skin graphed transplant??
Tattoo over skin graft
Feb 20, 2020
It is possible to tattoo over a skin graft. Whether it will hold the ink nicely and be a good result may be a different story. If the graft has healed smoothly you might be okay. Ask your tattoo artist to give you an honest opinion. Best, Dr. M

Tattoo Regret
Feb 20, 2020
Sorry to hear you’re unhappy with your tattoo. Almost all tattoos can be safely removed with laser. I recommend finding a PiQO4 device and an experienced provider. How many sessions would it take to remove my tattoo?
Tattoo Removal
Feb 7, 2020
Sorry to hear you are unhappy with your tattoo. The good news is that most tattoos can be removed. The bad news is that I would think this tattoo will take at least 8-10 treatments. Intracel and facial filler

Do Halo (and other lasers) thin the skin and therefore cause you to age faster long-term, in exchan
Lasers and antiaging
Jan 6, 2020
Thanks for posting your question. Actually, lasers will help improve the skin and slow down the aging process by building collagen. There is a wealth of scientific studies that show this can happen. Of course nothing can stop the aging process, but Laser can help reverse signs of aging and with good skin care (avoiding the sun and not smoking) you can keep a lot of the changes! Best of luck, Dr. M
BBL for big pores?
BBL for pore size
Jan 6, 2020
BBL is a fantastic device on many levels, but it is not great for pore size reduction. BBL will help even skin tone and remove sun damage and redness. It is also known to have anti-aging effects on the skin. Pores are better treated with radio frequency and micro needling. Best of luck, Dr. M 
Deep cellulite – BBL or Cellfina / Cellulaze?
Cellulite
Jan 6, 2020
Thanks for posting. Cellulite can be difficult to treat and almost always requires using various devices. Cellulite is a problem at 3 levels – the bands, the fat and the skin. I recommend using manual disruption to cut the bands (Cellfina or similiar) in conjunction with CelluSmooth to melt the “lump” of fat and tighten the skin. Best of luck, Dr. M
