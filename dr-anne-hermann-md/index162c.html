Would a lip flip make my lips bigger? What treatment do you suggest?
Lip flip vs Lip Filler
Aug 30, 2020
Hello,Typically, a lip flip is when a provider injects four to six units of Botox into the upper lip. This relaxed the muscles above the lip and can give the appearance of a subtle increase in volume. Usually, the lip flip is so subtle, that I recommend a dermal filler, such as Juvederm or Volbella if someone truly wants an increase in lip volume. A skilled injector can make the volume look natural and will likely give a better outcome.

Can Botox cause nose droop
Jan 14, 2020
Hello!
The good news is that the effects of Botox should only last 3 or 4 months. It should not make your nose droop. It sounds as if you might be experiencing a brow droop from the muscles of the forehead being over-relaxed. Perhaps the brow droop is making you notice the nose more. All of this should resolve and it would be good to return to your provider to see if they can help it resolve more quickly.
I'm trying to get rid of the excess skin that hangs under my arms. Can Kybella help?
Kybella for body fat and excess skin
Apr 19, 2018
Kybella does remove body fat in small areas, including under the arm. It will not, however, remove excess skin. If you feel that some of this hanging is fat, Kybella will help it, but you may want to pair the kybella with a skin tightening procedure. Best of luck.
I’m a kidney donor will Botox effect my one kidney?
Botox and kidney function
Apr 19, 2018
Botox is a local treatment and does not have any effect on kidney function. You should be fine if you choose to proceed.
Eight hours after Botox, I can already see results on my forehead. What does this mean?
Early Botox results
Mar 21, 2018
I have some patients who notice the affects of Botox as early as the next day. In general, it takes 3 days for the effects to begin and 10 to see the full effect. I would not worry if you see the effect earlier. That does not mean that it will wear off more quickly. Sometimes, especially the first time, it just kicks in more quickly than average. I hope you enjoy your treatment.
Can Botox cause damage to the baby in the early stages of pregnancy?
Is Botox Safe during pregnancy?
Mar 21, 2018
Please rest assured. Though we do not recommend Botox during pregnancy, it is a local treatment and is completely absorbed after a few hours. Because we do not study elective procedures on pregnant women, there are only small trials in which pregnant women received Botox. In those trials, Botox did not affect the baby. Congratulations on your pregnancy.
