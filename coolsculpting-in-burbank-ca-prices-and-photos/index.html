Coolsculpting In Burbank CA: Prices And Photos

Coolsculpting (fat splitting by cooling) is a novelty in the series of innovative developments of the main hospital of Massachusetts and Harvard University.

A group of scientists, based on a patented technique and clinical trials in hundreds of patients, proved that fat cells are extremely sensitive to cold, unlike other surrounding tissues. This technology is the basis of the unique device ZELTIQ.

Zeltiq® is the only method for removing fat deposits by cooling fat tissue.

The essence of the procedure is the targeted and high-precision effect of cold on fat cells (adipocytes) in problem areas in the abdomen, back, waist, hips and knees, while surrounding tissues remain intact.

Uniqueness of the device:

An Innovative Way To Contour Your Body By Freezing Unwanted Fat Away

  • Non-surgical skin tightening;
  • The newest technology of cryolipolysis;
  • Improved results compared with the cavitation and RF lifting;
  • For one procedure, up to 26% of fat is removed.

Patients may not worry because of the effects of the device on the body, because the use of the Zeltiq device completely excludes the effect of the device on the internal organs – only on the fat fold.

This device is approved by the FDA (Food and Drug Administration) in the United States, and is recognized to be 22.4% more effective than other hardware methods of medical cosmetology. After completing the course of 1-3 procedures, the fat tissue decreases due to the destruction of fat cells, after which they are eliminated from the body naturally.

How it works?

During the session, the vacuum nozzle captures a large crease of the skin along with the subcutaneous fat, not less than 2.5 cm thick (an “intelligent” device is so programmed that it will not work if the thickness of the fold is not sufficient for safe exposure).

Inside the applicator there are special plates that generate cold and provide deep cooling of the subcutaneous fatty layer. Between the applicator and the skin is placed a special gasket, which protects the skin from hypothermia and ensures uniform lipolysis. With the help of negative air pressure, the fat fold is captured and securely fixed, after which cold is applied to the treated zone.

CoolSculpting Is A Breakthrough, Non-invasive Procedure That Uses Cooling Technology To Noticeably Eliminate Fat Without Surgery

The patient has a feeling of cooling only at the very beginning of the procedure, but it soon passes. As already noted, the local skin temperature drops to about +25 ºÐ¡, so the procedure is well tolerated (sensations are quite comfortable), which can not be said about fat cells, that do not like the cold.

Due to the fact that the session takes a long time, patients are offered to take in advance an interesting book, a laptop, a smartphone, in order to spend pleasantly one to four hours.

In extreme cases, you can conveniently sit in a sitting or lying position and just take a nap – the device will do everything itself. The creators of cryolipolysis claim that the duration of the session and, correspondingly, the accompanying boredom is the only cons of the method.

CoolSculpting Technology Uses A Body Contouring Method Called Cryolipolysis

Depending on the thickness of the subcutaneous fat and the client’s wishes, one to three procedures are performed at intervals of two months. For two or three sessions, you can lose 6-10 cm of volume at the waist, 4-8 cm – on the hips and abdomen.

In total, about 30% of adipose tissue in a particular zone is removed (something still has to remain to maintain normal body functions).

We can say that the device Zeltiq is designed for busy people who want to look good, but are not ready to go to the table to a plastic surgeon or spend all their free time in the gym in the hope to overcome stubborn centimeters on the abdomen and waist.

And yet it should be understood that coolsculpting is aimed specifically at local correction of problem areas and will be ineffective with total excess weight.

In this case, you will first have to get rid of the general obesity with the help of proper diet and regular exercise. Unfortunately, miracles do not happen and a magic pill, from which disappear 15-20 extra pounds, has not yet been invented.

At one time, you can process a 20 × 20 cm zone, the session itself can last from one to four hours, that is, during this time, several problem areas are exposed to the effects.
