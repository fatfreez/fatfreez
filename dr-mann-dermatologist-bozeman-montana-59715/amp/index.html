Does Laser hair removal after hydrafacial cause wrinkles?
Laser Hair Reduction and Wrinkles
Jul 8, 2019
Hello,
Laser Hair Reduction will not promote or cause wrinkles after a Hydrafacial. It is very important to diligently apply sunblock daily and to avoid extended periods of direct sunlight before and after any laser treatment to avoid any potential hyperpigmentation. Staying well hydrated by drinking plenty of water and moisturizing daily will help keep your skin looking healthy and hydrated.
