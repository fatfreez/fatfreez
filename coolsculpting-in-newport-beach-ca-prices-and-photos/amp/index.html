Coolsculpting In Newport Beach CA: Prices And Photos

Freezing fat cells has gone from being a childhood peculiarity to a bonafide fat removal procedure. It all started when some scientists noticed that children would tend to lose cheek fat after sucking on popsicles.

This was called “popsicle panniculitis,” which essentially means the fat in the cheeks would become inflamed after eating popsicles or chewing on ice – the action would actually freeze fat cells.

This panniculitis was causing the fat cells to die, which seemed to have no ill effects aside from the loss of the fat cells.

You see, fat freezes at a fairly high temperature. Because of this, the fat in your body can undergo cell death due to freezing while every other cell in your body, even those in direct contact, remain perfectly fine.

You might get a little red in the area, perhaps become a little inflamed, and even get numb, but once everything returns to a comfortable temperature you won’t even notice the effect.

The dead fat cells, however, will then be removed from your body by the process called “efferocytosis.” They will be removed naturally and you will notice weight loss in the area in which this has occurred.

CoolSculpting Helps To Shape The Appearance You Want

You shouldn’t attempt to conduct this procedure yourself though. There is a machine called a Zeltiq machine which is used by doctors in a very controlled environment that can do this procedure with minimal discomfort. This process is called Coolsculpting.

This machine essentially pulls the fatty area of your body (usually the love handles and other trouble areas) and surrounds it with a cold pack that slowly lowers the temperature to a temp that will kill fat cells but leaves all other cells unharmed.

This procedure can take as litte as half an hour and can be done on your lunch break. The best part is that it requires no surgery so you avoid all of the associated complications and risks. There have really been few reported side effects at all.

CoolSculpting Patient Experience

Once your fat cells have been frozen, your body will undergo the efferocytosis process in order to remove them. This doesn’t happen quickly though, and it can take up to 6 weeks to see a noticeable difference. You might even need to have this procedure done several times to sculpt your body to exactly where you want it.

Freezing fat cells is not for everyone . You should be already at or near your ideal body weight, as indicated by the Coolsculpting website here. If you fit the profile, however, go ahead and consult your nearest Coolsculpting professional and freeze fat cells to sculpt your perfect body.
