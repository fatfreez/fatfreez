Would you like to get rid of fat deposits without pain, without a long and difficult rehabilitation period, without complications?

The dream becomes a reality due to the unique procedure of cryolipolysis this is the splitting of fats under the influence of cold.

Fatty folds leave completely painlessly – you just need to wait a little: the effect will manifest after the final destruction of fat cells, a month or two after the procedure.

Coolsculpting is a gentle effect on fat tissue and a powerful effect!

Features of cryolipolysis

In the process of Coolsculpting, cold exposure to a specific area of the body, where there are fat folds, local fat deposits:

A Coolsculpting Procedure May Eliminate The Stubborn Areas Of Fat That Many Women And Men Accumulate

  • Stomach;
  • Hips;
  • The internal surface of the hands;
  • Sides;
  • Back;
  • Buttocks.

A special apparatus is used with a nozzle provided with cooling panels, into which a fat fold is pulled under the influence of vacuum. Reliable equipment allows you to monitor the cooling level with great accuracy.

Coldness, a small numbness – that’s all that you feel. And your body at this time begins to work actively! Under the influence of low temperature, it naturally breaks down fats, thus securing energy to protect the “freezing” area. And this process of cryolipolysis does not die out after the procedure, but grows.

Thus, fat folds are reduced by an average of more than 25%! After achieving such an impressive result, you can achieve more by repeating the procedure in a couple of months. During one procedure – 45 minutes, the doctor can process a zone of about 10×18 cm.

BYE-BYE FAT CELLS

After the procedure, sometimes there is slight itching, redness in the area of exposure, but they quickly pass and do not cause discomfort. No side effects and a long period of rehabilitation!

The incredible benefits of Coolsculpting:

  • Strong and pronounced result after 1 procedure;
  • Reduction of fat in the treated area to 25% after 4 weeks after 1 session;
  • Gentle technique, not associated with skin disorders;
  • Absolute safety and painlessness;
  • Complete absence of unwanted side effects, risks and complications;
  • No need for rehabilitation and hospitalization;

Contraindications to Coolsculpting

Coolsculpting Involves A Hand-held Device That Features A Vacuum Pressure And Cooling Technique

  • Increased pathological sensitivity to cold (cold urticaria);
  • Circulatory disorders in the area of exposure to cold (blood clotting pathology, thrombosis, etc.), the use of anticoagulants;
  • Recent trauma in the treatment area, including with the damage of blood vessels (bleeding, hemorrhage);
  • Open and / or infected wounds, inflammatory processes, recent surgical intervention in the treatment area;
  • Skin diseases;
  • Pronounced skin lesions (extensive scar) or subcutaneous tissue (hernia) in the affected area;
  • Neuropathic disorders;
  • Pregnancy and lactation;
  • Herniated in the area of impact
  • The patient has an electrocardiostimulator.
