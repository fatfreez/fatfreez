Is cryolipolysis effective on muscular calves?
Treating calves with CoolSculpting
Mar 19, 2019
CoolSculpting is a great technology, but as a surgeon who does both a significant amount of CoolSculpting and liposuction, I would not recommend CoolSculpting for the lower legs. It is very likely to leave an indentation. Lower legs are best treated with liposuction by a surgeon experienced in lipo, as they can be very difficult to obtain a nice result on. I recommend seeing a board certified plastic surgeon to see if you are a candidate. Best of luck to you!
