In such complex areas as the abdomen, thighs, back and chin area, the inner side of the shoulders and the patella area – it is extremely difficult, and sometimes it is completely impossible to remove fat deposits by conservative methods.

Exercise the gym and diet are virtually powerless.

Today, the opportunity to get rid of fat deposits in such difficult, “problem” areas without radical types of intervention can be done with the help of Coolsculpting.

Afely, Comfortably, And Effectively

Cryolipolysis successfully eliminates so-called “fat traps” – local accumulations of fatty tissue – without pain, the risk of complications and without the need for a long rehabilitation period.

The essence of Coolsculpting

Adipocytes are adipose tissue cells – very sensitive to low temperatures. The direct effect of cold depresses their vitality and they are eliminated from the body naturally (apoptosis occurs – the physiological death of cells).

As a result, an effective reduction (up to 25%) of subcutaneous adipose tissue in the affected area is already performed in one procedure. In our clinic, cryolipolysis is carried out using a modern technical novelty – equipment of the American company Zeltiq Aesthetics – a unique device ZELTIQ.

Advantages of Coolsculpting

CoolSculpting Can Significantly Reduce The Appearance Of Unsightly Bulges

  • it is carried out without carrying out of punctures and cuts, that is, without damage of integuments and soft tissues of the patient;
  • The procedure is painless, therefore does not require anesthesia;
  • well tolerated, does not cause adverse reactions, has a minimum of contraindications (their presence can always be identified in advance by a doctor’s consultation);
  • It does not require special training is performed on an outpatient basis;
  • the procedure provides a prolonged effect – the process of “burning” fat continues for another 1-2 months.

In order to consolidate and increase the effect, it is recommended to carry out coolsculpting for two to three sessions, each with an interval of a couple of months.
